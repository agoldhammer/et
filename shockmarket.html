Change We Need

The Biden administration can reduce gun violence by authorizing these changes today to save lives tomorrow:

Establish an Office of Gun Violence Prevention

So we can streamline the government's efforts to reduce violence and establish a long-term, sustained effort to reduce gun deaths.

Invest in Community Violence Intervention Programs

Evidence-based programs address the root causes of gun violence in our communities and help disrupt cycles of interpersonal gun violence.

Crack Down on Illegal Firearm Trafficking

The Biden administration must nominate a new permanent director to head the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives (ATF) to focus on reducing firearm trafficking and saving lives.

Use the Presidential Bully Pulpit

Because the President needs to talk with Americans about the risks and dangers of firearm ownership within home and communities.
